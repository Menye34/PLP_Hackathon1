Food Delivery System Project

The Food Delivery System was a project given in school in the Software Development Tools course by our lecturer, Dr. Vallery. We were tasked with creating a system that would enable customers to interact with different local restaurants.

The group number was Group 13, made up of 5 members.

To build this system, our lecturer provided us with a guideline to follow, and each week we presented feedback through a PowerPoint presentation based on the topics in the guideline.
